ADVICE

What was the last topic that someone asked for your advice on?

I was asked by a youngman whether he should follow his father's wishes or his own. In some respects this was a hard one. It required much discussion and investigation. I asked him many questions to see where he thought his father was coming from? I asked him what partial conclusions he had come to? I then related events in my life in which I thought one thing and then later realized that my father was correct. I also passed on some examples in which my father has told me that maybe his methods, although well intended, may not have been the best.
