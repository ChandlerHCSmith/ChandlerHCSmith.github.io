Experience of the Day

My wife and I have a "tradition" of eating Thai food and watching one of our favorite shows, Survivor! But first:


Most people know what the game Tetris even looks like. Many people have played the game. However, not as many people have heard that there is a psychological phenomenon named in its honor. The idea goes as follows, many students played Tetris for several hours each day, even the scientist played Tetris for several hours each day. They noted that after a few days of game play, participants acknowledged having a similar response. The world around them started to look a bit like Tetris. As a car pulled into a mostly empty parking some subjects relayed having mentally placed the "object" into one of the available parking stalls with a sense of strategy, as though some parking stalls were of more value due to their physical relation to other full or empty stalls.


Wikipedia Article regarding the Tetris Effect
