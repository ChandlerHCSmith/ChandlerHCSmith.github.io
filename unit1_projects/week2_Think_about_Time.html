Time Management

What is your biggest time management challenge with during Phase 0?

I actually do pretty well with time management, however I work during the day and I work asynchronously for a university - this means I am almost always on.

I go to work each day until 5:00. I get home around 5:15 - short commute time is awesome! I jump on the computer. I then eat dinner with the family. I then check up on my uni-students. I then jump back on the computer. I can stay focused on a topic for long periods of time. Truthfully, I am having the hardest time with all of the, for me, new ins-n-outs of all the new accessories: +Google, Communities, Hangouts, Pairing, GPS, GitHub(did I only synch it to myself or also share it with the instructors.)

How will you combat that?

I will succeed by never giving up, by working with others, and by taking the necessary time to relax and regroup.

What techniques will you try?

When I hit a wall, I find that getting up and walking around and thinking through the problem helps. I think the physical break as well as the actual walking helps shift gears in my mind.

I also find that reading something challenging, but non-Program related, like Philosophy, helps me "recenter/relax". I think that this is because it requires me to stop.

Physical excerise is very helpful. I am more productive.

What is your overall plan for Phase 0?

Work, Program, Family, Program, Individual Non-program time.

Just keep swimming... :)
